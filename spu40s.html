Introduction:

Unlock the full potential of oil extraction with the remarkable SPU40S. This exceptional press is specifically designed to handle oilseeds of varying hardness, guaranteeing outstanding performance every time. With its robust drive and unparalleled strength, the SPU40S effortlessly squeezes oil from a wide range of challenging oilseeds.What sets the SPU40S apart is its ability to operate continuously, ensuring a seamless oil extraction process. Say goodbye to interruptions and maximize your productivity with this reliable companion by your side.

The SPU40S boasts an impressive capacity, capable of processing between 5-20 kg/h of raw material. Whether you're a small-scale producer or an industrial operation, the SPU40S adapts to your needs, providing efficient oil extraction on demand.

Equipped with a powerful 4kW drive electric motor, the SPU40S offers you the flexibility to control the speed according to your preferences. Adjust the extraction process to achieve the desired results, giving you complete control over the oil extraction experience.
